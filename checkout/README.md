# Checkout App

In this Next.js Shadcn app customers will be taken to a single page application with a product in their cart. Here the user can optionally choose to enroll in a subscription with a checkbox. Then they can provide billing information like their name, email, address, phone number. Then they can provide their payment information: card number, expiry date, CVV. Once they click checkout and there are no errors they should be taken to a checkout success page.
